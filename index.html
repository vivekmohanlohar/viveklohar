01. About Me

Hello! My name is Vivek and I enjoy engineering things that power both the physical and digital world. My interest in electrical systems started back in 2019 during my early student days when I first began tinkering with basic circuit components — turns out, figuring out how a tiny current flows through a path sparked a deep curiosity about how large-scale power infrastructure operates.

Fast-forward to today, and my engineering path has evolved from hands-on laboratory setups during my diploma to designing advanced power architectures for my Bachelor’s degree. Instead of just studying formulas out of a textbook, I focus my energy on where classical electrical engineering meets modern digital automation and sustainable power grids.

Rather than sticking strictly to a traditional academic route, I spend my time actively exploring clean energy alternatives, smart distribution models, and industrial-grade software design tools to build solutions that are practical, efficient, and future-proof.
